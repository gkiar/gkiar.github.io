Hi there, I'm Greg Kiar.

I am a Research Scientist at the Child Mind Institute, where I focus on evaluating and improving the trustworthiness of tools and techniques used to study the brain.

I apply computational statistics, uncertainty quantification, pipeline development, and machine learning towards understanding and leveraging the numerical uncertainty in neuroimaging software.

The goal of my work is to increase the transparency of published results as well as the generalizability and robustness of biomarkers we derive from them.
