Hey there, I'm Greg Kiar and I develop software tools for lowering the barrier for entry to performing and evaluating reproducible science.

I trained as a biomedical engineer, and work as an informatician and pretend neuroscientist. Since beginning my Ph.D. in the Montreal Neurological Institute at McGill University, I have focused on evaluating the trustworthiness and stability of tools in neuroimaging, in an effort to increase both the transparency surrounding results and the robustness of biomarkers we wish to derive from them.
